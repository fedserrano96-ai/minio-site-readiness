Building Division

  • Building Permit Services
  • Handouts and Forms
  • SJPermits.org

Welcome to the Building Division. We work to ensure the safety of buildings on private property as we apply the state of California's current building codes and laws to our review and inspection of projects.  We also oversee the Development Services Permit Center and online permitting at www.SJPermits.org.

  • Get started. View Start Your Project and with a click of a button, you will find instructions for your specific type of project.   

  • Plan Review is taking a little longer. Due to a high volume of applications and staff vacancies, the time frame for an initial review and subsequent reviews are currently longer than normal. See Plan Review Time Frames for the average time required and please add 2 to 3 weeks to these time frames for your project planning purposes. We appreciate your patience as we seek additional staff support. 

NEWS & announcements

Proposed New Fees and Proposed Changes to Existing Fees Related to Development Projects
As part of the budget process, the City Council will hold a series of Study Sessions and Council Meetings, May 6-11, 2026 to discuss and receive public comments on the proposed budget for fiscal year 2026-2027, including the proposed new fees and proposed changes to existing fees related to development projects.

The City Council will have two public hearings on May 12 beginning at 1:30 p.m. and June 8 beginning at 6 p.m. at which the public can provide comments on both the proposed budget and changes to existing fees related to development projects.

For more information, see the public hearing notice.

 

As of Jan. 1, 2026, the 2025 Building Codes are in effect. View the codes at Building Standards Commission.

Begin your building permit journey with Start Your Project. We want your permit journey to be as easy as possible. Now you can click a button that matches your project to find clear instructions for when your project requires a building permit, how design considerations affect your permit pathway, how to apply, and tips for a successful inspection. Attention Building Professionals! Enroll in our new Best Prepared Designer Program and you'll get building permits in as little as 3-5 days for your projects. Learn about who can apply and how the program works at www.bit.ly/SJ-BPDProgram.
