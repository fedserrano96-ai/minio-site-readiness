\n C.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Applicability. The provisions of this Section apply to all uses that will be used by a person with\n disabilities. In order to make housing and commercial or industrial facilities accessible\n to a person with disabilities, any person may request a reasonable accommodation,\n or exception to the City's Zoning Code regulations, in accordance with this Section.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n D.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Application and Review Procedure.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n 1.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Applicant. Any person who requests reasonable accommodation, based on the disability of residents\n or users, in the application of a land use or zoning law which may be acting as a\n barrier to fair housing and access opportunities, may submit an application. The applicant\n may be the person with the disability, or his or her representative, or a developer\n of housing or commercial or industrial facilities for the purpose of accommodating\n persons with disabilities.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n 2.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Application. An Administrative Design Review (ADR) application shall be filed with the Planning\n and Community Development Director, or his or her designee. In addition to completion\n of the ADR application, the applicant shall include:\n

\n \t\t\n

\n a.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Basis for the claim, such as a medical certification, that the person on whose behalf\n the accommodation is sought is disabled; or\n

\n \t\t\n

\n b.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Explanation of why the reasonable accommodation is necessary to make the specific\n housing or commercial facility accessible to the person with disabilities.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n c.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Other relevant information as requested by the Planning and Community Development\n Director, or his or her designee, in order to make the required findings.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n 3.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Noticing. The application noticing shall be consistent with Subsection 23.08.035.B. (Administrative\n Design Review) of the DMMC.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n 4.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Findings. Any decision on an application under this Section shall be supported by written findings\n addressing the criteria set forth below. An application under this Chapter for a reasonable\n accommodation shall be granted if all of the following findings are made:\n

\n \t\t\n

\n a.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n The housing or commercial facility, which is the subject of the request, will be used\n or occupied by a person with disabilities.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n b.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Due to the physical attributes of the subject property or the structures on-site,\n the requested reasonable accommodation is necessary to make the specific housing or\n commercial facility accessible to a person with disabilities under the Federal Fair\n Housing Act, the California Fair Employment and Housing Act, and the Americans with\n Disabilities Act (ADA).\n

\n \t\t\n

\n c.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n The requested reasonable accommodation would not create an undue financial or administrative\n burden for the City.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n d.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n The requested reasonable accommodation would not require a fundamental alteration\n in City's land use and zoning ordinances, programs or policies. In making this finding,\n the decision-making body may consider, but its consideration is not limited to, the\n following factors:\n

\n \t\t\n

\n i.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Whether the proposed changes to the subject property and structures, would adversely\n impact the health, safety or use of adjacent properties or the City right-of-way.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n ii.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Whether any reasonable alternatives have been identified that would provide an equivalent\n level of benefit without requiring a reasonable accommodation or exception to the\n City's applicable rules, standards and practices.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n 5.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Conditions of Approval. In granting a request for reasonable accommodation, the reviewing authority may impose\n conditions to ensure that the reasonable accommodation complies with the findings\n required by this Chapter. Conditions may also be imposed to ensure that any removable\n structures or physical design features that are constructed or installed in association\n with the reasonable accommodation be removed once those structures or physical design\n features are no longer necessary in order to accommodate a person with disabilities.\n The reviewing authority may require the recordation of the conditions of approval,\n or its equivalent.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n 6.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Determination. A determination timeframe on the application request shall be made consistent with\n Subsections 23.08.035.B., C., and D. (Administrative Design Review) of the DMMC.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n 7.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n Appeals. The appeal process shall be made consistent with Subsections 23.08.035.B., C., and\n D. (Administrative Design Review) of the DMMC.\n

\n \t\t\n
