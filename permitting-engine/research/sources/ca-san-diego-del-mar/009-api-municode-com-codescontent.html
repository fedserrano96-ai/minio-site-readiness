The Citizens' Participation Program is also intended to give the applicants\n and their agents the opportunity to understand and respond to concerns raised about\n the project's potential impacts on the community.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n B.\n

\n \t\t\n

\n The Citizens' Participation Program is not intended to produce complete consensus\n on all applications but to facilitate dialogue among applicants and neighbors early\n in the development review process. Completion of the Citizen's Participation Program\n shall not be construed as any prejudgment, commitment, or guarantee that an application\n for a Design Review Permit will be approved with or without any particular conditions.\n Approval of a Design Review Permit is at the sole discretion of the Design Review\n Board or City Council on appeal.\n
